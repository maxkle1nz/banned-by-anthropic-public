# BANNED BY ANTHROPIC

BANNED BY ANTHROPIC is a public archive of documented cases from builders and users affected by Anthropic enforcement actions.

This repository is the **public institutional face** of the campaign.

It exists to make the record:
- visible
- citable
- versioned
- harder to dismiss as rumor

## What lives here

- campaign framing and public purpose
- publication methodology
- privacy boundaries
- public snapshots and aggregate stats
- official updates
- press/research reference material

## What does NOT live here

- the intake backend
- private reports or raw exports
- moderation internals
- admin tooling
- infrastructure or secrets

## Principles

- truth over hype
- privacy over spectacle
- public numbers must be explainable
- private cases can count without being exposed
- publication must resist spam, impersonation, and abuse
