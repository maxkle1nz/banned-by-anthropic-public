# Publication Policy

BANNED BY ANTHROPIC aims to preserve a factual public record without turning publication into an abuse surface.

## Publishable material

Material may be published when it meets the campaign's public criteria and does not create disproportionate privacy or safety risk.

## Non-publishable material

Material should not be published when it includes:
- spam
- impersonation
- doxxing
- threats
- malicious deception
- clearly unsafe private detail

## Moderation principle

Public visibility is not all-or-nothing.

A case may be:
- public
- private but counted
- temporarily hidden during abuse review

## Why this matters

The campaign is stronger when the record stays credible.
