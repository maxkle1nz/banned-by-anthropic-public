# Manifesto

BANNED BY ANTHROPIC exists because scattered private harm is easy to ignore.

People lost access. Projects were disrupted. Workflows built around third-party harness usage were cut off or destabilized. In many cases, the consequences remained private, fragmented, and difficult to document in one place.

This campaign turns those fragments into a public record.

Not rumor. Not outrage theater. A record.

Our goal is simple:
- document what happened
- make patterns visible
- protect people who do not want full public exposure
- preserve a factual archive that journalists, researchers, and builders can cite

This is not an invitation to harass anyone.
This is not a dump of raw allegations.
This is not a replacement for evidence.

It is a disciplined public ledger of cases, bounded by publication rules, privacy limits, and moderation standards.

The point is visibility with integrity.
